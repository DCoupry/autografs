### Major findings during Step 2 (all fixed, see commit 405ffe7)
- **52% of RCSR nets were silently dropped** (1511/2931): RCSR writes
  I4132, pymatgen wants I4_132; suffixes :2/:H unknown to the encoding
  table. Normalization rescues ~1245 nets. ~266 remain: 2D plane groups
  (p6mm etc., ~200 — needed for hcb/COF layer nets, backlog) and
  nonstandard monoclinic settings (Cmca, I12/m1, P121/n1..., backlog).
- **Origin-choice trap**: SpaceGroup('Fd-3m:2').symbol and .int_number
  both silently drop the setting; feeding them back to from_spacegroup
  generates the net in origin choice 1 → garbage (dia: 64 wrong slots
  vs 24 right ones). Normalized symbol string must be passed through.
- **Point-group misdetection at unit scale**: RCSR arms ~0.25 A vs PGA
  tolerance 0.1 A → dia nodes typed O instead of Td. Fixed by running
  PGA on centered unit-normalized copies.
- **'# EDGE_CENTER' lines in RCSR files are commented out but the
  parser's l[2:] strip deliberately uncomments them** — that is where
  all 2-c slots come from. Preserve this behavior when refactoring.
- **Radial-mismatch objective (openissue → Step 3+5)**: slot dummies sit
  at blueprint quarter-edge positions; SBU dummies at physical radii.
  The RMSD objective mixes direction and arm-length error, so
  refine_cell=True on pcu+MOF-5 SBUs converges to an anisotropic cell
  (21 x 8.3 x 8.4 A) with overlapping atoms instead of cubic ~12.9 A.
  MOF-5 golden test asserts bookkeeping only until steps 3+5 land;
  tighten it then.

### Step 3 knowledge
- 2-c SBUs can never fail directional matching (two arms from their own
  centroid are always antiparallel); shape gates need 3+ connections.
- The SBU library's dummy-overlap convention gives MOF-5 at 12.77 vs
  experimental 12.9 A; a bond-length-aware pair target (offset by the
  X-neighbor distances) is a possible refinement (backlog).
- initial_scales() is analytically exact for uninodal isotropic nets;
  NM refinement mostly polishes multinodal/anisotropic cases.
- Autografs() init is now ~19 s with the 2464-net library — SBU PGA
  analysis + JSON reconstruction; lazy topology loading is the next
  perf item (backlog, Step 7).
